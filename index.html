Who am I?

My name is Kathleen and I am a Software Engineer. I have a background working in the food industry as a food scientist/laboratory technician in some of the major food companies in the area. My 5+ years of experience working in the food industry have given be a love for solving problems which I continue to do and love as a software engineer. Outside of coding I am very active in the anime community as a panelist and a volunteer/staffer, love video games, and enjoy working out and training as a newbie powerlifter in the USAPL. I really enjoy programming because it lets you express yourself in a weird way, even when you're not very creative, and to me its kind of like a puzzle and you get to think outside of the box when creating something new. I love expressing myself and showing off things that I love through all my coding projects, so take a look around! :) and check out my resume!
